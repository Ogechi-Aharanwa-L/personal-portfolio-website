About Me

From Finance Operations
to Full-Stack Development

I spent years in financial services operations — managing processes, spotting inefficiencies, and delivering under pressure. That experience taught me something powerful: the best systems are the ones you don't notice because they just work.

But I kept asking — "How do these systems actually get built?" My curiosity about the digital infrastructure behind modern finance led me to web development. Now I'm building that foundation — HTML, CSS, and beyond — with a clear goal: create practical, user-focused tools that solve real problems.

What drives me isn't just writing code. It's the moment a user interacts with something I built and thinks, "That was effortless." Small design decisions shape big experiences — and I'm here to craft every one of them with intention.
